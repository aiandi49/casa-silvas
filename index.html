Casa Silvas

A private one-bedroom guest suite with its own entrance, full kitchen, and full bath — tucked into a quiet Tucson neighborhood.

🚪 Private Entrance 🍳 Full Kitchen 🛏 Sleeps 2–4 🚗 Free Driveway Parking

Casa Silvas is a fully furnished guest suite built into a private Tucson home — its own bedroom, its own bathroom, its own kitchen, and its own front door, so you come and go without ever crossing paths with the main house.

It's been quietly hosting guests off-platform for the better part of a year — long enough for the details to get worked out: fresh linens on every bed, a kitchen that's actually stocked, and a host who answers fast. We're bringing it fully public so more travelers can find it, book it directly, and check us out first.

The House Rule

You will not go hungry here.

The kitchen isn't a courtesy microwave and a mini-fridge — it's a full working kitchen, stocked before every stay, because the whole point of a guest suite is that you can actually live in it for a few days.

  • Full-size range, oven, and microwave — cook a real meal, not just reheat one
  • Coffee maker and a Keurig, plus starter coffee, tea, and sugar
  • Cookware, dishes, and utensils for up to four people
  • Snacks and welcome basics stocked before you check in
